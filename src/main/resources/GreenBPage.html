Our Mission:

At WHO Database, our mission is to provide statistics about vaccines and infections, in a very clear and concise manner. We aim to provide an alternative to cluttered and hard-to-read datasets, so anyone can have easy access to helpful and insightful information.

How To Use Our Website?

At the top of our website, you can see four buttons, apart from homepage and About Us. These buttons will lead you to pages, where you can access our datasets. The first two buttons will lead you to pages where you can view our data broadly. The second two will allow you to have a deeper analysis of our data.

Each page will have a series of drop-down menus, that will allow you to select the data you want to see, as well as how you want that data ordered on some pages. Then just push a button to present, and there you go, all the data you want to see will be shown on a neat table.

As simple as that!
